Livemate

OVERVIEW
Livmate is a mobile app for people living in cities to find the right room with preferred conditions and roommates who they feel comfortable sharing their co-living space with.

As a design lead of this project team, I led the end-to-end design process from user research to pixel perfect visuals. I have also built design systems for efficient workspace and better collaboration with other designers.
