Blue Endeavor

OVERVIEW
As one of the client request projects at Young & Hungry agency, I was in charge of conducting a redesign of the existing Blue Endeavor app. Blue Endeavor was our client, and they wanted us to pursue their business needs and do a visual refresh of their existing design.
ROLE
Design Lead, Visual designer
TEAM
Manni Li (Wireframing), Xiaoyu (Icon design)
TEAM DYNAMICS
Working process
Collaboration
• My role: Team leader, design lead, product manager
• Work routine: We had multiple meetings to discuss the product requirements, client requests, and visual direction.
Timeline
• Created a timeline for the team to work based on the schedule for the client.
• This helped us utilize the time more efficiently, which was especially important in the agency environment where we worked on tight time span.
DESIGN GOAL
Client Needs

--Current design

Visual Request
• Want to have consistent and scalable design.
• Want to have more modern style UI.
• Want to have color patterns that are appropriate for their dark theme interface.
Business needs
• Increase user engagement: Add features that can increase user retention.
• Highlight company's mission: Educate users what Blue Endeavor is doing as an organization.
• Promote Deep Discussion: Promote new business model.
VISUAL
Color for dark theme
Color systems
• I updated Blue Endeavor's app color systems that is more applicable for the dark theme.
• The old design didn't have clear guidelines for the use of color, so I also provided the usage guidelines.
Design components
What I improved
• Scalable design: I created a set of design components that can be used across their product.
• Visual hierarchy: Added visual hierarchy to the existing content to help increase readability of the information.
PRODUCT
Suggested solutions
Increase user engagement
• By showing the number of friends that are going to the event, it encourages users wanting to participate in the event.

• Gamification and score dashboard allow users to become more proactive and motivated in participating in the organization.
Highlight company's mission
• Added BE’s mission statement and relative images in the splash screen to emphasize the company’s mission.

• Reiterate BE’s mission in the top section of the main home page.
Promote Deep Discussions
• By putting the new feature “Deep Discussion” at the upper carousel increase users attention leading to higher number of clicks.

• Added user review section to allow users to get access to others feedback on this events.

CONCLUSION
What I learned
How to manage a design team
Managing a team was not easy. Being a co-founder of this project team, I was involved in recruiting other team members, organizing team tasks and schedules, and creating a deck for every meeting. I learned how to communicate my ideas effectively and also to give and receive feedback. Since our team worked remotely, I learned a lot of tools to help us communicate and collaborate from different places.
Importance of documentating and organizing work
Livmate was a long-term project and we had multiple iterations from initial sketch to final prototype. Therefore, it was important to organize and manage our design files in different versions and history. This also helped us when we hand off our design files and explain our design iterations to other teammates.
