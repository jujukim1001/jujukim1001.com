CONCLUSION
What I learned
Designing in an agency setting
In a fast-paced environment like design agency, it was important to produce final deliveries very quickly. Being adjusted to such setting, I created visual components first to make the design process much effectively and apply changes quickly if needed. We did a lot of design crit sessions, and had to show multiple visual explorations, so having a component library really helped a lot.
Aligning different visual styles
In our design team, we had a lot of talented visual designers with different design styles and approaches. In order to align different visual directions, we did a lot of meetings and sync ups to explore different visual styles and make decisions that everyone would feel involved. Also, we had to show different versions of visual design, so we have broken up into four teams (two designers each) and produced different versions to show for our client to choose one.
